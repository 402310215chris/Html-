Admission Requirements for HICT

  • A National Senior Certificate with an endorsement of admission to a Higher Certificate and/or Diploma and/or Degree: or
  • A National Certifiacte Vocational (NCV) at NQF Level 4 within an associated field and level of achiecements or:
  • Mature Age Exemption for students above the age of 23 as set out in the Higher Education Regulations: or
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)
